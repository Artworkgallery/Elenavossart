About Elena

For over 20 years, I've been painting the walls of cities, homes, and public spaces with murals that don't just decorate—they speak.

My work blends bold color, intricate detail, and deep emotional resonance. Whether it's a 50-foot urban wall or an intimate interior piece, every mural is a collaboration between the space and the story it deserves to tell.
